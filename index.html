Hi there! You're now the proud owner of a tech game that features augmented reality. Let's look at how it works.

Break into teams in any way
you like. There must be at least
two people per team.

All teams receive cards with
AR labels (unique symbols).
Team members have to decide
who shows the images
and who tries to guess them.
Every round, you switch roles.

The player who scanned
the AR label has 30 seconds
to show the image from
the card to their team without
saying any words.

If the team guesses the image,
they earn 1 point.

If the time is up, the game
automatically moves to the
next image. The team doesn't
get a point.

If the player doesn't know how
to show the image, they can tap
the "cross" button to move
to the next one. In this case,
the team doesn't get a point.

The winner is the team that guesses the most images.
